## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Data Categories Used | Legal Basis (for GDPR) |
|---|---|---|
| **Lead Matching & Booking** | Business identity, contact info, service areas, job info | Contractual necessity |
| **Payment Processing** | Financial data, banking info, tax info | Contractual necessity |
| **Ghost Agent Improvement** | Communication data, job info, job site photos | Legitimate interest |
| **Identity Verification** | Personal identity, license/insurance data | Legal obligation |
| **Tax Reporting** | Tax identification numbers, payment records | Legal obligation |
| **Customer Support** | All data categories | Contractual necessity |
| **Fraud Prevention** | Technical data, identity data, license data | Legitimate interest |
| **Platform Analytics** | Anonymized usage data | Consent (where required) |
| **Service Communications** | Contact information (email, phone) | Contractual necessity |
| **Legal Compliance** | All data categories | Legal obligation |

**Specific Use Cases:**

- **Lead Matching:** We use Contractor service area, trade, availability, and pricing data to match End Customer service requests with the appropriate Contractor. End Customer contact information is shared with the matched Contractor exclusively for the purpose of performing the Booked Job.

- **Ghost Agent Operations:** Our AI Ghost Agents use job descriptions, service area data, pricing schedules, and historical booking data to respond to End Customer inquiries, qualify leads, and book jobs. Ghost Agents may access Contractor availability and scheduling data to automatically set appointments.

- **Payment Processing:** Banking and financial data is transmitted to Stripe for payment remittance. JobSure does not store full payment card numbers. All financial data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

- **Agent Improvement:** De-identified job data, customer interactions, and booking outcomes are used to continuously improve the accuracy and effectiveness of the Ghost Agent system. No personally identifiable information is used for agent training without separate consent.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Encryption

- **Data in Transit:** All data transmitted between the Platform and users is encrypted using **TLS 1.2+** (Transport Layer Security).
- **Data at Rest:** All stored data is encrypted using **AES-256** encryption at the storage layer.
- **Payment Data:** Payment card information is tokenized and stored by **Stripe**, a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processor. JobSure does not store full card numbers, CVV codes, or PINs.

### 6.2 Access Controls

- **Role-based access control (RBAC):** JobSure employees and contractors have access to personal data only on a need-to-know basis.
- **Multi-factor authentication (MFA):** Required for all administrative access.
- **Audit logging:** All access to personal data is logged and monitored for unauthorized activity.
- **Zero-trust architecture:** Network segmentation, least-privilege access, and continuous verification.

### 6.3 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects, JobSure will:
1. Notify affected individuals within **72 hours** of discovery (or as required by applicable law);
2. Notify relevant supervisory authorities (e.g., ICO, state Attorney General);
3. Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach.
