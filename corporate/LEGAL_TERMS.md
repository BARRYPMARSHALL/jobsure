## 2. Service Description

2.1 **Ghost-Managed Lead Generation Network.** JobSure operates an autonomous AI-powered lead generation and booking platform designed specifically for independent skilled trades contractors. The Platform uses Ghost Agents to:

- Respond to customer inquiries 24/7/365 via phone, web chat, SMS, and email;
- Qualify leads based on job scope, location, budget, and timing;
- Schedule appointments and book service jobs directly into the Contractor's calendar;
- Collect and verify End Customer information for each Booked Job;
- Process initial service scoping and standard pricing estimates.

2.2 **Territory Exclusivity.** Subject to payment of the monthly Retainer, the Contractor is granted an exclusive service Territory. No other Contractor will be offered the same Territory during the term of this agreement. The boundaries and service parameters of the Territory are defined in the Contractor's onboarding profile and may be adjusted by mutual agreement.

2.3 **No Guarantee of Minimum Leads.** JobSure uses commercially reasonable efforts to generate leads and Booked Jobs within the Contractor's Territory. However, JobSure does **not guarantee** a minimum number of leads, Booked Jobs, or revenue. Lead volume depends on market conditions, seasonal demand, End Customer behavior, and other factors outside JobSure's control.

2.4 **Platform Availability.** JobSure commits to 99.5% uptime for the Ghost Agent system and Contractor dashboard, measured monthly. Scheduled maintenance, force majeure events, and third-party service interruptions are excluded from this commitment.

## 3. Fee Structure

3.1 **Monthly Retainer.** The Contractor shall pay a fixed monthly Retainer of **$[Retainer Amount]** (the "Retainer") for access to the Platform, Territory exclusivity, and Ghost Agent infrastructure. The Retainer is due on the first day of each calendar month and is non-refundable, except as provided in Section 8.

3.2 **Service Fee.** For each Booked Job facilitated through the Platform, the Contractor shall pay a Service Fee of **15% (fifteen percent)** of the total job value billed to the End Customer (the "Service Fee"). The Service Fee is calculated on the gross job amount before any Contractor expenses, material costs, or taxes.

3.3 **Fee Examples:**
- A Booked Job with a total value of $500 incurs a Service Fee of $75.
- A Booked Job with a total value of $1,200 incurs a Service Fee of $180.
- A Booked Job with a total value of $3,500 incurs a Service Fee of $525.

3.4 **No Per-Lead Fees.** The Contractor is **never charged** for individual leads, inquiries, estimates, or quotes. Fees are incurred exclusively on Booked Jobs as defined in Section 1.

3.5 **Fee Adjustments.** JobSure reserves the right to adjust the Service Fee percentage and Retainer amount upon **30 days' written notice** to the Contractor. Fee adjustments apply only to Booked Jobs that occur after the effective date of the change.
